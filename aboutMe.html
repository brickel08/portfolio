Drums

I'm a computer programmer by day, touring drummer by night! I started playing drums in 2001. Since then, drumming has taken me across the country - and more. I currently play in three different touring bands. The Rope, Guggenz, and Artifakts. I have taught drum lessons. I also do audio production and engineering for The Rope. I manage merchandising, bookings, itineraries, and financial guarantees for The Rope.

Sports

I love watching and playing sports. I consider myself to be a competitive person. I love all Minnesota sports teams, but my favorite team by far is the Minnesota Vikings. I watch every single game every season - it's something I really look forward to. My entire family is Vikings fans, and we have fun bonding over games. I also enjoy playing golf and disc golf in the beautiful Minnesota weather. I participate in a couple different fantasy football leagues with some buddies and old coworkers as well. I'm improving and really enjoy keeping track of players' stats.

Videogames

Similar to football, videogames have brought my family together for decades. We love Nintendo products and characters, and we regularly play videogames together. Some of our favorites include Super Mario, Zelda, and Pokemon. My brother and I, who also happens to be a software developer, have grown up playing videogames with our friends and family, and we both really love the bonding experiences it has brought us.
